J-Pop

Upbeat and catchy Japanese pop music, known for its energetic melodies and vibrant production.

Featured Artists

Fujii Kaze

Japanese singer-songwriter and pianist who debuted in 2019 and gained fame with his viral hit "Shinunoga E-Wa." His first album, Help Ever Hurt Never, topped Japan's Billboard charts.

Yoasobi

Japanese music duo known for turning short stories into hit songs, debuting in 2019. Their biggest hits include "Yoru ni Kakeru" and "Gunjou," both massive streaming successes.

Kenshi Yonezu

Japanese singer-songwriter and former Vocaloid producer who debuted in 2012 and gained fame for his unique blend of pop and rock. His most popular songs are "Lemon" and "Peace Sign," widely celebrated for their emotional depth.
