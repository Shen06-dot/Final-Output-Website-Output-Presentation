J-Rock

Energetic Japanese rock featuring bands with powerful guitar riffs, drums, and dynamic performances.

Featured Artists

Asian Kung-Fu Generation

Japanese alternative rock band formed in 1996, known for blending  Western and Japanese rock. Their popular songs include "Haruka Kanata"  and "Rewrite," which featured as anime openings.

Buck-Tick

Japanese rock band formed in 1983, influential in the visual kei  movement. They are known for songs like "Dress" and "Aku no Hana."

Kana-Boon

Japanese rock band formed in 2008, popular for energetic songs and anime themes. Their hits include "Silhouette" and "Full Drive."
