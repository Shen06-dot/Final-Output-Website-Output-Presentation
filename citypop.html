City Pop

Smooth, jazzy Japanese pop from the 70s–80s that captures the urban, laid-back vibes of city life.

Featured Artists

Miki Matsubara

Miki Matsubara was a Japanese city pop singer, debuting in 1979. Famous  songs include "Mayonaka no Door (Stay With Me)" and "Neat na Gogo  San-Ji".

Mariya Takeuchi

Japanese singer-songwriter known for her city pop genre contributions  since the late 1970s. Famous songs: "Plastic Love," "September" .

Tatsuro Yamashita

A key figure in Japanese city pop since the 1970s. Famous works include "Ride on Time" and "Love Talkin'" .
